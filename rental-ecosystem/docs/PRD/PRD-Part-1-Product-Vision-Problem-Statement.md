# Product Vision & Problem Statement
## Rental Ecosystem Platform — PRD Part 1

---

## 1. Product Vision

To build a trusted, end-to-end rental ecosystem that transforms how people in Kenya discover, connect to, and manage rental homes by eliminating physical house hunting and introducing a secure digital home connection system.

The platform will ensure that tenants can find and move into verified homes digitally, while landlords and agents can efficiently manage properties, tenants, and rental operations in one unified system.

---

## 2. Core Idea

Instead of tenants physically searching for houses, the platform enables them to:

- **Discover** available homes digitally
- **Request access** using a unique Home ID
- **Get verified** and approved by landlords or agents
- **Move into** homes with a secure digital connection

Once connected, tenants transition from **"house hunting mode"** to **"living mode,"** where they can manage rent, maintenance, and communication.

---

## 3. Problem Statement

The current rental process in Kenya is **fragmented, inefficient, and highly manual**:

- Tenants must physically visit multiple locations to find houses.
- Listings are often outdated, unverified, or duplicated.
- Communication between tenants, landlords, and agents is inconsistent.
- There is no standardized system to confirm legitimate occupancy.
- Rent payment and tenancy tracking are mostly offline and informal.
- Tenants have no structured digital support after moving in.

---

## 4. Solution Overview

This platform solves these problems by introducing:

- A **verified property ecosystem**
- A secure **Home ID-based connection system**
- **Digital approval** and verification workflows
- **Structured tenant onboarding** into properties
- **Post-move-in rental management** tools
- **Audit logging** for all critical actions

---

## 5. Key Principles

- **Trust first:** verification of landlords, agents, and tenants
- **Single source of truth:** each property has a unique identity (Home ID)
- **Lifecycle-based design:** Discover → Connect → Live → Move
- **Mobile-first** experience for tenants
- **Web-based management** for landlords and agents
- **Security and auditability** in all transactions

---

## 6. Success Outcome

### A tenant should be able to:

- Find a home without physically visiting multiple locations
- Move into a verified property with minimal friction
- Manage their entire tenancy digitally

### A landlord/agent should be able to:

- Manage properties and tenants efficiently
- Reduce fraud and unauthorized occupancy
- Track rental operations in real time
